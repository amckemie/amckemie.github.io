What I Do

With MakerSquare's project-driven, independent-learning focus, I worked on developing critical object-oriented and test-driven development skills. Throughout the course I worked on numerous projects (individually and with partners), allowing me to work on the following full stack web development skills: Ruby, Relational Databases (SQLite and PostegreSQL), ActiveRecord, Git, APIs, Algorithms (Sorting, Searching, Tree Traversal), Javascript, Rails, Rake, Foundation, CSS3, and HTML5.

Who Am I?

A 25-year-old lover of books, running, swimming, and dogs, I was a full-time marketing/SEO content editor before I finally gave into the unyielding urge to program. I love learning new things, meeting new people, and experiencing new things. I also enjoy giving back to the community and regularly volunteer with Big Brothers, Big Sisters and Back on My Feet.

Senioritis. If only there was enough time at MakerSquare to feel senioritis. Sigh.
